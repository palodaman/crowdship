What is CrowdShip?

CrowdShip is the package delivery app that makes sending out-of-town packages easy, secure, and affordable, by connecting you with trusted drivers and gig workers already traveling to that destination. Say goodbye to high courier fees and slow delivery times—CrowdShip offers a fast, flexible, and hassle-free alternative that’s both budget-friendly and eco-conscious. Try CrowdShip today and enjoy special promotions for first-time senders and drivers!

Features

Below are the features that CrowdShip currently offers.
Sign In/Sign Up

The sign-up and sign-in process is fully secure, using authentication methods to verify each user’s identity. This feature ensures that only verified individuals can participate, either as senders or drivers. CrowdShip is a safe environment that protects you and your personal information. New users can sign up with confidence, knowing that the CrowdShip community is verified.

Send Packages

Senders can easily send packages, and with inputs for pickup time, and special instructions, senders don't have to worry about coordinating a thing! CrowdShip also generates suggested prices based on package size, taking the hassle and worry out of choosing an appropriate rate, though you can still negotiate. CrowdShip makes sending a package straightforward, customizable, and convenient.

Find Deliveries

This page is specifically designed for drivers, providing an intuitive way to browse available delivery requests along their route. Our advanced algorithm prioritizes packages located near their final destination, enabling drivers to efficiently identify deliveries that align with their journey. By focusing on deliveries closest to their endpoint, drivers can optimize their trips, and complete multiple deliveries in one go, thus enhancing convenience, boosting earnings, and contributing to a reduced environmental footprint.

Accept Deliveries

Upon selecting a delivery, the driver has the option to accept it. Each listing includes details package details to allow drivers to select suitable deliveries that match their planned travel route, maximizing earning potential with minimal deviation.

My Deliveries

My Deliveries puts drivers in full control of their deliveries. Drivers can view their current delivery assignments, start and complete deliveries with ease, and communicate directly with senders through an integrated chat feature. All delivery details are readily accessible for a seamless experience. The dashboard also provides a comprehensive history of past deliveries, helping users track their completed jobs. This feature streamlines the entire delivery process, offering organization, transparency, and efficiency in one convenient interface.

My Packages

My Packages keeps senders in control of their deliveries at every stage. Users can track packages that haven’t shipped, monitor in-progress deliveries, and review completed shipments, all in one place. Real-time updates on delivery status ensure transparency, while the ability to edit delivery details or discard packages adds flexibility. The integrated chat feature also allows seamless communication with drivers, ensuring clarity and responsiveness throughout the process. Plus, there is a complete history of past deliveries. This feature makes managing packages organized, intuitive, and stress-free.

Chat

This page lists all ongoing deliveries for both senders and drivers, providing real-time updates, tracking information, and estimated time of arrival. This feature offers peace of mind with full visibility into the delivery’s status.

Review

The Ratings and Reviews System fosters a trusted and reliable community on CrowdShip. Both drivers and senders can rate and review each other after a delivery, providing valuable feedback and promoting accountability. These ratings are used to vet users, ensuring that only reputable senders and drivers remain active on the platform. This feature helps maintain a high standard of service, encourages positive interactions, and builds confidence among users, making CrowdShip a trusted choice for hassle-free and reliable deliveries.

FAQ

1. How does pricing work?

CrowdShip uses a transparent pricing model based on factors like distance, package size, and delivery urgency. Since drivers are already heading to the destination, our rates are often lower than traditional courier services. While you have the ability to set your own prices, CrowdShip recommends prices that are fair for both the driver and sender.

2. Is CrowdShip safe to use?

Absolutely! Safety is our top priority! All users go through a secure sign-up process with identity verification, and both senders and drivers are rated after each transaction. Additionally, our insurance policy cover lost of damaged packages for added peace of mind, and our in-app messaging allows you to communicate with your chosen driver directly.

3. How reliable is CrowdShip?

CrowdShip’s community-driven model prioritizes reliability and trust. We encourage users to select drivers with high ratings and positive reviews, and our real-time tracking helps ensure deliveries stay on schedule. In the rare case of a delay or issue, our support team is available to help resolve it quickly.

4. What can I expect as a new user?

As a new user, you’ll find the app straightforward to navigate. Whether you’re a sender or a driver, you can quickly set up your account, explore available deliveries, and read user reviews. First-time senders and drivers may also be eligible for special promotions. Our team is always here to help if you have any questions!

5. Are there any limitations on what I can send?

For safety and compliance reasons, CrowdShip has guidelines on what can and cannot be sent. Items like hazardous materials, illegal substances, and oversized or extremely heavy packages are restricted. Feel free to reach out if you’re unsure about a specific item, we're more than happy to help!

6. What happens if my package isn’t delivered as expected?

In the unlikely event of an issue with your delivery, CrowdShip’s support team is ready to assist. Your lost goods are covered by our insurance policy, and our customer service team is available to help with any concerns, delays, or adjustments to delivery plans.

7. How do reviews and ratings work?

After each delivery, both the sender and driver can leave a rating and review. These reviews are used by senders so that they know their package is in safe hands, and they're used by drivers to make informed decisions. They are also used by CrowdShip to vet drivers and keep the community safe.

8. How does CrowdShip minimize environmental impact?

By connecting packages with drivers already on the road, CrowdShip reduces the need for additional delivery vehicles and excess emissions. We’re proud to offer an eco-friendly alternative to traditional courier services, helping to lower the carbon footprint of each delivery.
