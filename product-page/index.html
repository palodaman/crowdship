Affordable, convenient, hassel-free community package delivery

What is CrowdShip?

CrowdShip is the package delivery app that makes sending out-of-town packages easy, secure, and affordable by connecting you with trusted drivers and gig workers already traveling to your destination. Say goodbye to high courier fees and slow delivery times—CrowdShip offers a fast, flexible, and hassle-free alternative that’s both budget-friendly and eco-conscious. Try CrowdShip today and enjoy special promotions for first-time senders and drivers!

Features

Below are the features that CrowdShip currently offers.
Sign In/Sign Up

The sign-up and sign-in process is fully secure, using authentication methods to verify each user’s identity. This feature ensures that only verified individuals can participate, either as senders or drivers. CrowdShip is a safe environment that protects you and your personal information. New users can sign up with confidence, knowing that the CrowdShip community is verified, which reduces the risk of misuse.

Request Deliveries

Senders can easily create a new delivery request, providing details like pickup and drop-off points, delivery timing, and special instructions. CrowdShip generates suggested prices based on package size, taking the hassle and worry out of choosing an appropriate rate, though senders and drivers can still negotiate. They can then post the request for drivers to accept. CrowdShip makes creating a delivery request straightforward, customizable, and convenient. This feature helps secure faster matches with drivers while ensuring clarity about delivery requirements.

Find Deliveries

This page is designed for drivers and drivers to browse available delivery requests along their route. Each listing includes details like the pickup and drop-off locations, delivery timeframe, package size, and sender rating. This allows drivers to efficiently select deliveries that match their planned travel route, maximizing earning potential with minimal deviation. This feature supports flexible scheduling and helps drivers find multiple deliveries to complete in a single trip, increasing convenience and income while reducing environmental impact.

Accept Deliveries

For drivers and gig workers, this page lists available delivery requests, including details like pickup and drop-off locations, delivery timeline, and package details. While prices are fair, drivers and senders can negotiate to find a rate that works best for both parties. Be empowered to choose deliveries that align with your routes and schedules, maximizing earning potential without extra hassle. This flexibility also enables more efficient and eco-friendly delivery.

My Deliveries

My Deliveries puts drivers in full control of their deliveries. Users can view their current delivery assignments, start and complete deliveries with ease, and communicate directly with senders through an integrated chat feature. All delivery details, including pickup and drop-off locations, timelines, and package specifics, are readily accessible for a seamless experience. The dashboard also provides a comprehensive history of past deliveries, helping users track their completed jobs and earnings. This feature streamlines the entire delivery process, offering organization, transparency, and efficiency in one convenient interface.

My Packages

My Packages keeps senders in control of their deliveries at every stage. Users can track packages that haven’t shipped, monitor in-progress deliveries, and review completed shipments, all in one place. Real-time updates on delivery status ensure transparency, while the ability to edit delivery details or discard packages adds flexibility. An integrated chat feature allows seamless communication with drivers, ensuring clarity and responsiveness throughout the process. All package details, including pickup and drop-off locations, timelines, and specific instructions, are easily accessible. Plus, a complete history of past deliveries provides valuable insights for future planning. This feature makes managing shipments organized, intuitive, and stress-free.

Chat

This page lists all ongoing deliveries for both senders and drivers, providing real-time updates, tracking information, and estimated time of arrival. This feature offers peace of mind with full visibility into the delivery’s status.

Review

The Ratings and Reviews System fosters a trusted and reliable community on CrowdShip. Both drivers and senders can rate and review each other after a delivery, providing valuable feedback and promoting accountability. These ratings are used to vet users, ensuring that only reputable senders and drivers remain active on the platform. This feature helps maintain a high standard of service, encourages positive interactions, and builds confidence among users, making CrowdShip a trusted choice for hassle-free and reliable deliveries.

FAQ

1. How does pricing work?

CrowdShip uses a transparent pricing model based on factors like distance, package size, and delivery urgency. Since drivers are already heading to the destination, our rates are often lower than traditional courier services. While you have the ability to set your own prices, CrowdShip recommends prices that are fair for both the driver and sender.

How we calculate pricing:

1. Basic fee — We have a minimum starting price of $5

2. Volume — Charges are based on package size

    Small parcel (L x W x H < 10,000): $2

    Medium parcel (10,000 ≤ L x W x H < 30,000): $5

    Large parcel (30,000 ≤ L x W x H < 50,000): $10

    Oversize (L x W x H ≥ 50,000): $20 + $10 oversize surcharge

3. Distance pricing — Charges are based on delivery distance

    Within 20km: $0.15/km

    Over 20km: $0.20/km

    Fixed Ferry Fee: $15 for inter-regional routes (e.g. Victoria to Vancouver).

Example 1: Small parcel (L 30 cm x W 20 cm x H 15 cm = 9,000, delivery distance 10 km)

    Base Fee: $5

    Volume Fee (small parcel): $2

    Distance Fee: 10 km x $0.15 = $1.50

    Total cost: $5 + $2 + $1.50 = $8.50

Example 2: Medium Parcel (L 40 cm x W 25 cm x H 25 cm = 25,000, delivery distance 25 km)

    Base Fee: $5

    Volume Fee (Medium Parcel): $5

    Distance Fee: 25 km x $0.20 = $5.00

    Total Cost: $5 + $5 + $5.00 = $15.00

Example 3: Large Parcel (L 50 cm x W 30 cm x H 30 cm = 45,000, delivery distance 50 km)

    Base Fee: $5

    Volume Fee (Large Parcel): $10

    Distance Fee: 50 km x $0.20 = $10.00

    Total cost: $5 + $10 + $10.00 = $25.00

Example 4: Extra Large Parcel (L 70 cm x W 40 cm x H 40 cm = 112,000, delivery distance 100 km)

    Base Fee: $5

    Volume Fee (Oversized Parcel): $20

    Distance Fee: 100 km x $0.20 = $20.00

    Oversized Package Surcharge: $10

    Total Cost: $5 + $20 + $20.00 + $10 = $55.00

Example 5: Medium Parcel + Boat Ferry Inter-Regional (L 40 cm x W 25 cm x H 25 cm = 25,000, Delivery Distance Victoria - Victoria Dock + Vancouver Dock - Vancouver = 70 km)

    Base Fee: $5

    Volume Fee (Medium Parcel): $5

    Distance Fee: 25 km x $0.20 = $14.00

    Boat Ferry Fee: $15

    Total Cost: $5 + $5 + $14.00 + $15 = $39.00

2. Is CrowdShip safe to use?

Absolutely! Safety is our top priority! All users go through a secure sign-up process with identity verification, and both senders and drivers are rated after each transaction. Additionally, packages can be covered by insurance for added peace of mind, and our in-app messaging allows you to communicate with your chosen driver directly.

3. How reliable is CrowdShip?

CrowdShip’s community-driven model prioritizes reliability and trust. We encourage users to select drivers with high ratings and positive reviews, and our real-time tracking helps ensure deliveries stay on schedule. In the rare case of a delay or issue, our support team is available to help resolve it quickly.

4. What can I expect as a new user?

As a new user, you’ll find the app straightforward to navigate. Whether you’re a sender or a driver, you can quickly set up your account, explore available deliveries, and read user reviews. First-time senders and drivers may also be eligible for special promotions. Our team is always here to help if you have any questions!

5. Are there any limitations on what I can send?

For safety and compliance reasons, CrowdShip has guidelines on what can and cannot be sent. Items like hazardous materials, illegal substances, and oversized or extremely heavy packages are restricted. Feel free to reach out if you’re unsure about a specific item, we're more than happy to help!

6. What happens if my package isn’t delivered as expected?

: In the unlikely event of an issue with your delivery, CrowdShip’s support team is ready to assist. We offer insurance on all deliveries, and our customer service is available to help with any concerns, delays, or adjustments to delivery plans.

7. How do reviews and ratings work?

After each delivery, both the sender and driver can leave a rating and review. These reviews are used by senders so that they know their package is in safe hands, and they're used by drivers to make informed decisions. They are also used by CrowdShip to vet drivers and keep the community safe.

8. Can I schedule a delivery in advance?

Yes! CrowdShip offers both real-time and scheduled deliveries. You can select a pickup time that works best for you, allowing you to plan around your schedule and find a driver who can deliver within your preferred timeframe.

By connecting packages with drivers already on the road, CrowdShip reduces the need for additional delivery vehicles and excess emissions. We’re proud to offer an eco-friendly alternative to traditional courier services, helping to lower the carbon footprint of each delivery.
