What is CrowdShip?

CrowdShip is the package delivery app that makes inter island delivery easy, secure, and affordable, by connecting you with trusted drivers and gig workers already traveling to that destination. Say goodbye to high courier fees and slow delivery times—CrowdShip offers a fast, flexible, and hassle-free alternative that’s both budget-friendly and eco-conscious. Try CrowdShip today and enjoy special promotions for first-time senders and drivers!

Features

Below are the features that CrowdShip currently offers.
Sign In, Sign Up, and Authentication

The sign-up and sign-in process is fully secure, utilizing advanced authentication methods to verify each user’s identity. This ensures that only verified individuals can participate, whether as senders or drivers. CrowdShip prioritizes your safety, safeguarding both you and your personal information. New users can join with confidence, knowing they’re becoming part of a trusted, verified community.

Splash Screen

Get started effortlessly as soon as you open the app. Want to be a sender? Jump straight into sending packages. Prefer to be a driver? Head directly to delivering packages. Wherever you need to go, the app gets you there with ease!

Send Packages

Sending packages is simple and hassle-free with CrowdShip. Senders can easily input the item name, pickup time, special instructions, and even add optional photos—no need for any additional coordination. CrowdShip also suggests prices based on package size and distance, eliminating the stress of determining an appropriate rate. For more details on how these estimates are generated, check the FAQ. Overall, CrowdShip offers a straightforward, customizable, and convenient solution for sending packages.

Send Packages — Price estimator

Don't know how to price an item, we're here to help with our estimator. Input the approximate package dimensions and weight and we'll calculate a price that is fair for both you and the driver.

Find Deliveries

This page is designed with drivers in mind, offering an intuitive way to browse available delivery requests along their route. Drivers simply input their starting and ending destinations, and our advanced algorithm prioritizes packages near their final destination. This makes it easy for drivers to identify deliveries that align with their journey. By focusing on packages closest to their endpoint, drivers can optimize their trips, complete multiple deliveries at once, and maximize earnings, all while minimizing their environmental impact.

Accept Deliveries

When selecting a delivery, drivers have the option to accept it or not. Each listing includes key information, allowing drivers to choose deliveries that align with their planned travel route, optimizing earning potential with minimal deviation. Drivers can also stack multiple deliveries to maximize efficiency in one trip. This feature streamlines the process, making it easy for drivers to accept deliveries that suit their schedule and route, ensuring a seamless and profitable experience.

Find Deliveries — Price negotiation

Drivers have the the option to accept the set price or negotiate a higher rate by selecting a custom price or choosing from 10%, 15%, or 20% price increases. If a driver opts to negotiate, the sender will receive a notification and can choose to accept, decline, or continue negotiating via the integrated chat.

Orders — My Deliveries

My Deliveries gives drivers complete control over their delivery tasks. Drivers can easily view their active assignments, start and complete deliveries, and communicate directly with senders via the integrated chat feature. All delivery details are readily accessible, ensuring a smooth and efficient process. My Deliveries also provides a detailed history of past deliveries, allowing drivers to track their completed jobs. This feature simplifies the entire delivery experience, offering organization, transparency, and efficiency in one user-friendly interface.

Orders — My Packages

My Packages gives senders full control over their deliveries at every stage. Users can easily track packages that are pending shipment, monitor deliveries in progress, and review completed shipments—all in one place. Real-time updates* on delivery status ensure transparency, while the option to edit delivery details or cancel packages adds flexibility. The integrated chat feature enables smooth communication with drivers, ensuring clarity and responsiveness throughout the process. Additionally, users have access to a complete history of past deliveries. This feature makes managing packages organized, intuitive, and hassle-free.

FAQ

1. How does pricing work?

CrowdShip uses a transparent pricing model based on factors like distance, package size, and delivery urgency. Since drivers are already heading to the destination, our rates are often lower than traditional courier services. While you have the ability to set your own prices, CrowdShip recommends prices that are fair for both the driver and sender.

2. Is CrowdShip safe to use?

Absolutely! Safety is our top priority! All users go through a secure sign-up process with identity verification, and both senders and drivers are rated after each transaction. Additionally, our insurance policy cover lost of damaged packages for added peace of mind, and our in-app messaging allows you to communicate with your chosen driver directly.

3. How reliable is CrowdShip?

CrowdShip’s community-driven model prioritizes reliability and trust. We encourage users to select drivers with high ratings and positive reviews, and our real-time tracking helps ensure deliveries stay on schedule. In the rare case of a delay or issue, our support team is available to help resolve it quickly.

4. What can I expect as a new user?

As a new user, you’ll find the app straightforward to navigate. Whether you’re a sender or a driver, you can quickly set up your account, explore available deliveries, and read user reviews. First-time senders and drivers may also be eligible for special promotions. Our team is always here to help if you have any questions!

5. Are there any limitations on what I can send?

For safety and compliance reasons, CrowdShip has guidelines on what can and cannot be sent. Items like hazardous materials, illegal substances, and oversized or extremely heavy packages are restricted. Feel free to reach out if you’re unsure about a specific item, we're more than happy to help!

6. What happens if my package isn’t delivered as expected?

In the unlikely event of an issue with your delivery, CrowdShip’s support team is ready to assist. Your lost goods are covered by our insurance policy, and our customer service team is available to help with any concerns, delays, or adjustments to delivery plans.

7. How do reviews and ratings work?

After each delivery, both the sender and driver can leave a rating and review. These reviews are used by senders so that they know their package is in safe hands, and they're used by drivers to make informed decisions. They are also used by CrowdShip to vet drivers and keep the community safe.

8. How does CrowdShip minimize environmental impact?

By connecting packages with drivers already on the road, CrowdShip reduces the need for additional delivery vehicles and excess emissions. We’re proud to offer an eco-friendly alternative to traditional courier services, helping to lower the carbon footprint of each delivery.
