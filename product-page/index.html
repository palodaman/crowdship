Affordable, convenient, hassel-free community package delivery

What is CrowdShip?

Crowdship is the package delivery app that makes sending out-of-town packages easy, secure, and affordable by connecting you with trusted commuters and gig workers already traveling to your destination. Say goodbye to high courier fees and slow delivery times—Crowdship offers a fast, flexible, and hassle-free alternative that’s both budget-friendly and eco-conscious. Try Crowdship today and enjoy special promotions for first-time senders and drivers!

Features

Below are the features that CrowdShip currently offers.
Sign In/Sign Up

The sign-up and sign-in process is fully secure, using authentication methods to verify each user’s identity. This feature ensures that only verified individuals can participate, either as senders or commuters. CrowdShip is a safe environment that protects you and your personal information. New users can sign up with confidence, knowing that the Crowdship community is verified, which reduces the risk of misuse.

Request Deliveries

Senders can easily create a new delivery request, providing details like pickup and drop-off points, delivery timing, and special instructions. They can then post the request for commuters to accept.CrowdShip makes creating a delivery request straightforward, customizable, and convenient. This feature helps secure faster matches with commuters while ensuring clarity about delivery requirements.

Find Deliveries

This page is designed for drivers and commuters to browse available delivery requests along their route. Each listing includes details like the pickup and drop-off locations, delivery timeframe, package size, and sender rating. This allows drivers to efficiently select deliveries that match their planned travel route, maximizing earning potential with minimal deviation. This feature supports flexible scheduling and helps drivers find multiple deliveries to complete in a single trip, increasing convenience and income while reducing environmental impact.

Accept Deliveries

For commuters and gig workers, this page lists available delivery requests, including details like pickup and drop-off locations, delivery timeline, and package details. Be empowered to choose deliveries that align with your routes and schedules, maximizing earning potential without extra hassle. This flexibility also enables more efficient and eco-friendly delivery.

Active Deliveries

This page lists all ongoing deliveries for both senders and commuters, providing real-time updates, tracking information, and estimated time of arrival. This feature offers peace of mind with full visibility into the delivery’s status.

FAQ

1. How does pricing work?

Crowdship uses a transparent pricing model based on factors like distance, package size, and delivery urgency. Because we connect senders with travelers already heading to the destination, our rates are often lower than traditional courier services. You’ll see an estimated cost before confirming your delivery, so there are no surprises!.

2. Is Crowdship safe to use?

Absolutely. Safety is our top priority. All users go through a secure sign-up process with identity verification, and both senders and commuters are rated after each transaction. Additionally, packages are covered by insurance for added peace of mind, and our in-app messaging allows you to communicate with your chosen commuter directly.

3. How reliable is Crowdship

Crowdship’s community-driven model prioritizes reliability and trust. We encourage users to select commuters with high ratings and positive reviews, and our real-time tracking helps ensure deliveries stay on schedule. In the rare case of a delay or issue, our support team is available to help resolve it quickly.

4. What can I expect as a new user?

As a new user, you’ll find the app straightforward to navigate. Whether you’re a sender or a commuter, you can quickly set up your account, explore available deliveries, and read user reviews. First-time senders and drivers may also be eligible for special promotions. Our team is always here to help if you have any questions!

5. Are there any limitations on what I can send?

For safety and compliance reasons, Crowdship has guidelines on what can and cannot be sent. Items like hazardous materials, illegal substances, and oversized or extremely heavy packages are restricted. You’ll find more details in our app, and feel free to reach out if you’re unsure about a specific item.

6. What happens if my package isn’t delivered as expected?

: In the unlikely event of an issue with your delivery, Crowdship’s support team is ready to assist. We offer insurance on all deliveries, and our customer service is available to help with any concerns, delays, or adjustments to delivery plans.

7. How do reviews and ratings work?

After each delivery, both the sender and commuter can leave a rating and review. These reviews help maintain a reliable community and provide insight into user experiences. High ratings and positive feedback make it easier to find trusted partners for future deliveries.

8. Can I schedule a delivery in advance?

Yes! Crowdship offers both real-time and scheduled deliveries. You can select a pickup time that works best for you, allowing you to plan around your schedule and find a commuter who can deliver within your preferred timeframe.

9. How does Crowdship contribute to sustainability?

By connecting packages with commuters already on the road, Crowdship reduces the need for additional delivery vehicles and excess emissions. We’re proud to offer an eco-friendly alternative to traditional courier services, helping to lower the carbon footprint of each delivery.
